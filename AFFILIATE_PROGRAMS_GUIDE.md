Amazon Associates is your **primary revenue source** because:

### Step-by-Step Registration

#### Step 1: Create Your Amazon Account

1. Go to [https://affiliate-program.amazon.com](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** (top right)
3. Use your existing Amazon account or create a new one
4. **Recommendation:** Use a business email (e.g., affiliate@acnelist.com)

#### Step 2: Fill Out Account Information

**Section 1: Account Information**
- **Payee Name:** Your legal name or business name
- **Address:** Your business address
- **Phone Number:** Your contact number

**Section 3: Website and Mobile App List**

- **Website URL:** `https://acnelist.com` (or your Manus domain)
- **Mobile App:** Leave blank (not applicable)

**Important:** Your website must be live and have content before applying!

**Section 4: Profile**

- **What topics does your website cover?**
  - Select: **Health & Wellness**, **Beauty**, **Product Reviews**
  
- **What type of Amazon items do you intend to list?**
  - Select: **Beauty & Personal Care**, **Health & Household**

- **How do you drive traffic to your website?**
  - Select: **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**, **Social Media**, **Email Marketing**

- **How do you usually build links?**
  - Select: **Product Reviews**, **Comparison Tables**, **Educational Content**

- **How many total unique visitors does your website get per month?**
  - **First month:** Select "0-500" (be honest)
  - **After 3 months:** You can update to "500-5,000"
  - **After 6 months:** Update to "5,000-50,000"

- **What is your primary reason for joining the Amazon Associates Program?**
  - Write: *"To monetize my skincare blog by recommending high-quality acne treatment products to my audience. I provide honest, research-based reviews to help people find effective solutions."*

- **How did you hear about us?**
  - Select: **Search Engine**

#### Step 3: Identity Verification

- Enter your **phone number**
- Amazon will send you a **PIN code**
- Enter the PIN to verify

#### Step 4: Payment and Tax Information

**Payment Method:**
- **Direct Deposit** (recommended - fastest)
  - Enter your bank account details
  - Routing number and account number
  
- **Amazon Gift Card** (alternative)
  - Minimum: $10
  
- **Check** (slowest option)
  - Minimum: $100

**Tax Information:**
- **US Residents:** Complete W-9 form
- **Non-US Residents:** Complete W-8BEN form
- Amazon will withhold taxes if required

#### Step 5: Complete Application

- Review all information
- Click **"Finish"**
- You'll receive an email confirmation

**Approval Timeline:** 24-48 hours (usually instant)

---

### Step 6: Make Your First 3 Sales (Qualification Requirement)

**Important:** Amazon requires you to make **3 qualified sales within 180 days** to keep your account active.

**How to Qualify:**
1. Share your affiliate links with friends/family

3. Publish your first blog articles
4. If you don't make 3 sales in 180 days, you'll need to reapply

**Tip:** Focus on low-priced items ($5-15) for easy first sales:

### Creating Affiliate Links

#### Method 1: SiteStripe (Easiest)

1. Log in to your Amazon Associates account
2. Visit any Amazon product page
3. You'll see a **black toolbar** at the top (SiteStripe)
4. Click **"Get Link"** ‚Üí **"Text"**
5. Copy the link and paste it in your website

**Example:**
```
https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0XXXXXX?tag=acnelist-20
```

#### Method 2: Product Linking (Recommended for Bulk)

1. Go to [Amazon Associates Dashboard](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com)
2. Click **"Product Linking"** ‚Üí **"Product Links"**
3. Search for the product (e.g., "CeraVe Foaming Cleanser")
4. Click **"Get Link"**
5. Choose **"Text Only"** or **"Image + Text"**
6. Copy the HTML code

**Example HTML:**
```html
<a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0XXXXXX?tag=acnelist-20" target="_blank">
  Buy CeraVe Foaming Cleanser on Amazon
</a>
```

#### Method 3: Link to Any Page

1. Go to Associates Dashboard
2. Click **"Product Linking"** ‚Üí **"Link to Any Page"**
3. Paste any Amazon URL
4. Click **"Go"**
5. Copy your affiliate link

---

### Best Practices for Amazon Links

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use descriptive anchor text: "Buy on Amazon" or "Check Price on Amazon"
- Add `target="_blank"` to open links in new tab
- Use `rel="nofollow sponsored"` for SEO compliance
- Track clicks with Google Analytics events

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Shorten links with bit.ly (violates Amazon TOS)
- Use misleading anchor text
- Click your own links (can get you banned)
- Share links on closed Facebook groups (violates TOS)

### Link Cloaking (Optional but Recommended)

**Why Cloak Links?**
- Cleaner URLs: `acnelist.com/recommends/cerave` instead of long Amazon link
- Track clicks easily
- Change destination without updating all pages
- Professional appearance

**How to Cloak:**

**Method 1: WordPress Plugin (if using WordPress)**
- Install **Pretty Links** or **ThirstyAffiliates**
- Create short links like `/go/product-name`

**Method 2: Custom Redirects (for Manus platform)**
- Create a redirect file in your project
- Map short URLs to affiliate links

**Example Code:**
```typescript
// client/src/utils/affiliateLinks.ts
export const affiliateLinks = {
  'cerave-cleanser': 'https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0XXXXXX?tag=acnelist-20',
  'paulas-choice-bha': 'https://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=XXXXXX...',
  'differin-gel': 'https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0XXXXXX?tag=acnelist-20',
};

export function getAffiliateLink(productId: string): string {
  return affiliateLinks[productId] || '#';
}
```

### FTC Disclosure Requirements

**You MUST disclose affiliate relationships** to comply with FTC guidelines.

**Where to Disclose:**
1. **Affiliate Disclosure Page** (already created: `/affiliate-disclosure`)
2. **On every page with affiliate links:**
   - Add disclosure at the top of blog posts
   - Add disclosure near affiliate links

**Example Disclosure:**
```
*This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission 
if you purchase through our links at no extra cost to you. 
All recommendations are based on research and genuine reviews.*
```

**Full Disclosure Example:**
```html
<div className="bg-amber-50 border-l-4 border-amber-500 p-4 mb-6">
```

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Issue 1: Amazon Associates Application Rejected

**Reasons:**
- Website not live or incomplete
- Insufficient content (need 10+ articles)
- Poor website quality
- Traffic from prohibited sources

**Solution:**
- Publish 10-15 quality articles
- Ensure website is fully functional
- Reapply after 30 days

---

#### Issue 2: No Sales After 180 Days (Amazon)

**Problem:** Amazon will close your account if you don't make 3 qualified sales in 180 days.

**Solution:**
- Share links with friends/family for initial sales
- Focus on low-priced items ($5-15)
- Promote on social media
- If account closes, you can reapply immediately

---

#### Issue 3: Low Conversion Rate

**Problem:** Getting clicks but no sales (conversion rate < 2%)

**Possible Causes:**
- Wrong audience (not interested in buying)
- Poor product recommendations
- Broken or incorrect links
- Slow website loading

**Solutions:**
- Improve product descriptions and reviews
- Add comparison tables
- Use high-quality images
- Test different CTAs
- Ensure links work correctly

---

#### Issue 4: ShareASale Application Rejected

**Reasons:**
- Website too new (< 30 days old)
- Duplicate content
- Poor website design
- Insufficient traffic

**Solution:**
- Wait 30-60 days and build content
- Ensure original, high-quality content
- Improve website design and UX
- Reapply after improvements
